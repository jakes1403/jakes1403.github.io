About personalized vs non‑personalized ads

Depending on your region and device settings, ads may be personalized using your device’s advertising identifier. You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android settings (see below). When personalized ads are off or not permitted, you may still see ads, but they should not be tailored using that identifier.

Your choices & controls

  • Opt out of ad personalization: On most Android devices, open Settings → Google → Ads (or Privacy) and adjust the advertising preferences, such as “Opt out of Ads Personalization” or “Delete advertising ID.” Exact wording varies by Android version.
  • Limit permissions: You can manage app permissions in your device settings. Indirection does not request sensitive permissions beyond what is necessary to display ads.

Children’s privacy

Indirection is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to our game through a third‑party ad, please contact us so we can work with our partners to address it.
